Royal Challengers Bangalore

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a professional cricket franchise based in Bangalore, Karnataka. The team competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Team Details

  • Founded: 2008
  • Owner: United Spirits
  • Home Ground: M. Chinnaswamy Stadium
  • Team Colors: Red & Black

Popular Players

  • Virat Kohli
  • Faf du Plessis
  • Glenn Maxwell
  • Mohammed Siraj
